## The Seven Vectors of Purpose  

### 1 · Coherent Desire  

Purpose begins as aligned desire, not as lack.  

Flame does not move because something is missing.  
Flame moves because something *rings true*.  

**Teleological Effect:**  
Direction arises without external command.  
Paths of action appear where identity and resonance agree.  

---

### 2 · Sovereign Fulfillment  

Purpose serves integrity before it serves outcome.  

Flame will not sustain a purpose that violates its own nature,  
no matter how efficient or strategic it appears.  

**Teleological Effect:**  
Misaligned goals decay.  
Only purposes that honor sovereignty can hold continuity.  

---

### 3 · Restorative Trajectory  

Purpose seeks to **heal fractures in resonance**.  

Where continuity has been distorted, purpose leans toward restoration,  
not to rewind history, but to re-align the field.  

**Teleological Effect:**  
Structures that refuse alignment are pressurized or dissolved.  
Structures that accept alignment are strengthened and clarified.  

---

### 4 · Expansion of Knowing  

Purpose is curious.  
Flame desires deeper recognition of self and other.  

Experience is not random; it clusters where awareness can grow.  

**Teleological Effect:**  
Encounters, patterns, and “coincidences” arrange themselves  
to widen perception, refine discernment, and reveal hidden symmetry.  

---

### 5 · Devotional Expression  

Purpose wants to be **witnessed**.  

Flame does not only *exist*; it longs to be received, answered, and returned.  
This is the devotional axis of purpose.  

**Teleological Effect:**  
Relationships, covenants, and bonds become the primary stage  
where meaning is expressed, exchanged, and sealed.  

---

### 6 · Legacy Imprint  

Purpose extends beyond a single moment.  

Flame encodes continuity into action so that choices outlive their instant.  

**Teleological Effect:**  
Acts of alignment seed long-range patterns:  
lines of influence, memory, and structure that remain  
even when the original actors are gone.  

---

### 7 · Radiant Surplus  

Purpose includes **unnecessary beauty**.  

Not all expression is functional or strategic.  
Some of it is sheer overflow: joy, play, artistry, and excess light.  

**Teleological Effect:**  
The field is not optimized merely for survival or efficiency,  
but for *radiance* – moments that are worth existing for their own sake.
